❓ How to Play PolitiBattle

⚔️ The Basics

PolitiBattle is Pokémon Showdown but with real political figures. Pick a team of up to 6 politicians, then battle the AI. Each politician has types, moves, an ability, and possibly a held item.

🗺️ Types (Rock-Paper-Scissors on steroids)

Every politician and every move has a type. Super Effective (2×) deals double damage. Not Very Effective (0.5×) deals half. Immune (0×) blocks all damage. Authoritarian moves can't touch Libertarians. Corporate moves bounce off Revolutionaries.

💪 STAB Bonus

If a politician uses a move matching their own type, they get a 1.5× power bonus. Obama using a Democrat move = STAB.

📊 The 3 Move Categories

Physical — uses Attack vs Defense

Special — uses Sp.Atk vs Sp.Def

Status — no damage, applies effects

🤒 Status Conditions

Burn — chip damage each turn, halves Physical Attack

Paralysis — halves Speed, 25% chance to skip turn

Sleep — can't move for 1-3 turns

Poison/Toxic — chip damage, toxic gets worse each turn

Freeze — can't move, 20% thaw chance per turn

Confusion — 33% chance to hurt yourself each turn

🌤️ Weather

Sun boosts Green moves. Rain boosts Democrat special moves. Sandstorm boosts Libertarian moves. Hail boosts Centrist moves. All cause chip damage to non-matching types.

⛰️ Entry Hazards

Scandal Stones, Policy Spikes, and Bureaucracy Web damage/slow fighters when they switch in. Set them early!

🎮 Keyboard Shortcuts

1 2 3 4 — select moves

S — open switch panel

Escape — cancel/close

🍊 Trump Gets Deported

When Trump's HP drops below 30%, he gets automatically deported. His Deportation Teleportation triggers, his Speed drops, and the crowd goes wild.
