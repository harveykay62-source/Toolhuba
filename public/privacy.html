Plain-English Summary: We collect only what we need to run ToolHub AI. We never sell your data. Students are fully ad-free. You can delete your account and all data at any time by contacting us.

1. Who We Are

ToolHub AI ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at toolhuba.onrender.com and all associated subpages, including multiplayer quiz features, the tool library, and user dashboards. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@toolhub.ai.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account data: name, email address, and password (stored as a one-way bcrypt hash — we cannot read your password).
  • Google Sign-In: if you use Google OAuth, we receive your name and email from Google. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Quiz content: quizzes and questions you create or submit.
  • Bug reports: any text you voluntarily submit through the bug-report form.
  • Payment information: PayPal handles all payments. We only store your PayPal transaction ID and subscription status — never card numbers.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage logs: which tools you use, timestamps, and page views — used for rate-limiting and analytics.
  • IP address: stored temporarily for rate-limiting; not linked to personal profiles.
  • Browser / device info: user-agent string used for aggregate analytics only.
  • Quiz play data: scores, streaks, and timing when you play quizzes.
  • Multiplayer session data: game room codes, player names used during a session, and final scores. Room data is temporary (Redis, auto-deleted after 4 hours); final scores are stored permanently.

2.3 What we do NOT collect

  • Your precise location (we only see a general country from your IP).
  • Your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • Browsing history outside of ToolHub AI.
  • Payment card details (all handled by PayPal).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to operate and improve ToolHub AI:

  • Authenticate you and keep your session active.
  • Enforce daily usage limits for free accounts.
  • Display your quiz history and scores on your dashboard.
  • Run multiplayer quiz sessions and record final leaderboards.
  • Detect and prevent abuse (rate-limiting, spam).
  • Send service emails (e.g., password reset) — no marketing emails unless you opt in.
  • Generate anonymous, aggregate analytics so we can understand feature usage.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on free accounts. AdSense may set cookies and use your browsing behaviour to show relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

  • Free accounts see ads by default.
  • Premium accounts are completely ad-free.
  • Student accounts (registered from an admin-approved school domain) are always ad-free, regardless of account tier. No behavioural advertising data is collected for student sessions.
  • Teacher accounts are ad-free.
  • Educator accounts are ad-free.

You can opt out of personalised Google ads at adssettings.google.com.

5. Special Protections for Students

We take student privacy seriously and comply with COPPA, FERPA, and similar regulations where applicable.

  • Student accounts are automatically assigned when you register with an admin-approved school email domain.
  • Students never see ads — behavioural ad tracking is disabled for student sessions.
  • Student accounts are not used for marketing.
  • Teacher accounts can view aggregate student quiz statistics only within their approved school domain. Teachers cannot see personal data outside their domain.
  • Admins can revoke school domain approvals at any time.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If a student under 13 is registered, their school or parent/guardian should contact us for immediate deletion.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited cases:

  • Service providers: Render (hosting), Neon/PostgreSQL (database), Redis (temporary game rooms). These providers process data only on our behalf and under confidentiality obligations.
  • Payment processor: PayPal, for processing premium subscriptions. We share only what PayPal needs to complete the transaction.
  • Legal requirement: if required by law, court order, or to protect our users' safety.
  • Business transfer: in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred — you will be notified.

Multiplayer leaderboard scores are visible to all players in a game session. Player nicknames in multiplayer are public within a session but are not indexed or retained with your account profile.

7. Cookies & Storage

We use the following cookies and storage mechanisms:

  • Session cookie (toolhub.sid): keeps you logged in. HttpOnly, Secure in production. Expires after 7 days of inactivity.
  • Theme preference: stored in localStorage only. Never sent to our servers.
  • Google AdSense cookies: only present for free accounts. You can block these with any ad blocker or via browser cookie settings.

We do not use fingerprinting, supercookies, or tracking pixels of our own. You can clear all ToolHub cookies by logging out.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 10) — we cannot recover your password.
  • All connections are served over HTTPS/TLS.
  • Session cookies are HttpOnly and SameSite=Lax.
  • Database credentials are stored as environment variables — never in source code.
  • All multiplayer answers are validated server-side; clients cannot submit fake scores.
  • API endpoints use rate-limiting to prevent brute-force attacks.

Despite these measures, no internet service is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Tool usage logs: retained for up to 12 months for analytics, then anonymised or deleted.
  • Multiplayer room data: temporary Redis data is auto-deleted after 4 hours. Final scores are retained indefinitely.
  • Page-view logs: retained for 90 days, then aggregated and anonymised.
  • Bug reports: retained until resolved, then deleted.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) you may have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data (you can update your name/email in Dashboard → Settings).
  • Deletion: request full deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: request your data in a machine-readable format (JSON).
  • Opt-out of ads: upgrade to Premium, or use a browser ad blocker.
  • Withdraw consent: delete your account at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@toolhub.ai with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

11. Children's Privacy

ToolHub AI is designed to be used in educational settings, including by minors when supervised by a teacher or school. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent or school-verified access.

If you are a school using ToolHub AI, you are responsible for ensuring appropriate parental consents are in place as required by your local law (e.g., COPPA in the US, GDPR in the EU). Student accounts from approved school domains receive enhanced privacy protections as described in Section 5.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account without consent, contact us at privacy@toolhub.ai for immediate deletion.
