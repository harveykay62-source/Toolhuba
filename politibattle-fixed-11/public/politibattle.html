❓ How to Play PolitiBattle

🗺️ The Premise

50 of history's most chaotic political figures have been ripped from their timelines and forced to fight in the PolitiBattle arena. Each fighter has a Type, stats, and 4 moves. Your job: pick a fighter and outlast your opponent.

⚔️ Battle Flow

  • Each turn you choose one of your fighter's 4 moves — or switch fighters.
  • Moves have PP (Power Points). When PP hits 0, that move is exhausted.
  • Reduce your opponent's HP to 0 to win the round.
  • First to knock out all 3 of the opponent's fighters wins the match.

🎭 Types & Effectiveness

  • 10 types: Populist, Elitist, Hawk, Dove, Scandal, Spin, Legal, Grassroots, Corporate, Chaos.
  • Type matchups follow the Type Chart — some deal 2×, some ½×, some 0× damage.
  • A fighter's own type gets a 1.5× STAB bonus when using a matching move.

📊 Stats Explained

  • HP — health pool. Reach 0 and the fighter is out.
  • ATK / DEF — physical offense and defense.
  • SPD — who moves first each turn.
  • SPC — special move power (scandal leaks, spin campaigns).
  • ELO — your personal skill rating. Rises with wins, drops with losses.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Check the Type Chart before picking your fighter — a good matchup wins fights before they start.
  • Save high-PP moves for key moments. Running dry turns the tide fast.
  • Status moves (Filibuster, Propaganda, Recess) can turn losing fights around.
  • Switch early — don't wait until your fighter is at 10% HP.
  • Online rank matches use the same rules; your ELO updates win or lose.

🏆 Ranked Mode
